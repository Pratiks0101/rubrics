Who We Are

Pioneering Excellence in Educational Consulting

Rubrics SoftCon Private Limited, Pune is a highly focused initiative, founded by experienced academicians and professionals towards excellence in education. Our objective is to develop innovative software and rendering consultancy services to ease educational institute in Self-Evaluation, NAAC Accreditation, Autonomy, Permanent Affiliation, National and International ranking, and overall quality improvement in education. For Rubrics founders, it all started because of three reasons: one, being teachers, urge to do something for the education field; two, to reduce the time of teachers in their day-to-day administrative work to make them free for research, development, and innovations in teaching & learning. three is the belief that innovation in education is nothing but the implementation of an Outcome Based Education System. With these reasons and a dream in heart, they started Rubrics Softcon Pvt. Ltd. in the year 2018. The beginning of the company was humble, but today it is known to the entire education fraternity. Rubrics Softcon Private Limited Pune, is associated with many institutions and universities aspiring for Quality initiative, Quality sustenance, and Quality enhancement. We aim to help institutes to define the standard processes for Planning, Monitoring, and Control for the overall development of students.
